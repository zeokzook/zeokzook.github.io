Astronaught

  • Post author By zeok
  • Post date 30 November 2023
  • Categories In C++, Unreal Engine
Itch.io

Completion Date: 22/09/2023
Project Duration: 1 Week

Summary

  • Astronaught is a Exploration Tower Defense game where you gather resources and explore with your spaceship during the day and gather resources to upgrade your ship. At night, fight enemies with your upgraded ship and gather BioFuel from dead enemies. The objective of the game is to reach the BioFuel goal and escape the planet.
  • Developed for Epic MegaJam 2023. Made in Unreal Engine 5.
  • Part of a team of 5 as one of the programmers
  • In charge of resource and building spawning system and resource gathering system

Languages/Programs used

Contributions

I developed the resource and building spawning system for the game. We wanted resource areas and buildings to spawn around the player. So, I made a system where in the beginning of the game, resource areas and buildings will spawn around the player in a circle around the spaceship. On a new day, old resource areas will despawn and respawn around the spaceship. Resource Areas will not spawn close around the spacesghip but will spawn outside of a certain radius that can be modified. On the gif that shows the early stages of resource spawning, the red debug sphere shows the area where the resource area won’t spawn and the green debug sphere is the extents of the spawn area.

After we got some meshes made by our artist and the terrain of the map done. Some additional changes need to be done. Since the terrain in our game is bumpy, the resource points will rotate accordingly based on the normal of the terrain. This is done by doing a line trace and getting the normals from that. The resource points that spawn are chosen between different meshes and the amount of resource they hold vary depending on the mesh. This is also easily editable by anyone by adding a new row in the Resource Area Data Table so we can have different meshes for Resource Areas that give different Resource Amounts.

The building spawning is also done in the same system with an added radius for the buildings to spawn outside the Resource Area spawning radius. Inside the buildings, Resource Areas will also randomly spawn. Each building is a blueprint that has box collisions to determine where Resource Areas will spawn. The type of resource spawned in the buildings can be tweaked as well. It is determined by a range of the resource amount. The number of Resource Areas being spawned in the buildings can also be tweaked. The buildings only spawn once for now as I ran out of time. It would have been great if the buildings do spawn more if you wander far away enough from the cluster of buildings so it really feels like there are more to explore. There are only two types of buildings at the moment but more could easily be added by duplicating one of the building blueprints and changing the mesh and variables depending on what we want. The only time consuming process would be adding the box components for resource area spawning.

The player resource gathering system was also made by me. It was simple as we were using the Gameplay Ability System (GAS) as all I had to do was make an ability that activates by pressing right click. The ability does a sphere trace around the player and collects resources as long as the player is holding the button. The collection stops when the player lets go of the button or the player goes out of range.
